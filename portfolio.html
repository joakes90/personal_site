Apple Support


Apple Support gives you personalized access to solutions for all of your Apple products and services. Apple Support can help you find a nearby store, book a reservation, and add a pass to Wallet for easy check-in.


  • SwiftUI
  • Combine
  • Swift Concurrency
  • Compositional layouts

SongBar


Songbar is a macOS application for monitoring and controllign audio playback for Apple Music and Spotify. This application uses and is a good resource to learn about


  • Scripting Bridge
  • NSDistributedNotifications
  • Swift Package Manager
  • King Fisher

Key Bound


Key Bound is a keybinding application designed for users of vintage and generic keyboards to use their function keys to perform the same actions as on Apple’s Magic Keyboard. The app uses


  • SwiftUI
  • NSEvent
  • Accessibility permissions

Loves Connect


Loves Connect is a fully native swift app that allows commercial drivers to purchase diesel from their cabs, check into showers and use Siri to check their loyalty status. Loves Connect was an early adopter of SwiftUI, and has ample test coverage facilitated by the VIP/clean swift design pattern.

J.B. Carrier 360


Carrier 360 is an application that allows owner operators and other small fleets participate in J.B. Hunt’s 360 market place. The app allows these carriers to find and bid on loads that are brokered through J.B. Hunt as well as mange related documents.
