J.B. Hunt Drive

Drive was an app for J.B. Hunt drivers to manage their workload while on the road. Built on the node based Appcelerator Titanium platform, it allowed drivers to make check calls, message their fleet manager up update their ETA.

Loves Connect

Loves Connect is a fully native swift app that allows commercial drivers to purchase diesel from their cabs, check into showers and use Siri to check their loyalty status. Loves Connect was an early adopter of SwiftUI, and has ample test coverage facilitated by the VIP/clean swift design pattern.
